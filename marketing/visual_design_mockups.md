## 🎨 Color Palette & Branding

### Primary Colors:
- **Deep Navy**: #0D1117 (GitHub dark)
- **Bright Orange**: #FF6B35 (Call-to-action)
- **Pure White**: #FFFFFF (Text/contrast)

### Secondary Colors:
- **Success Green**: #4CAF50 (Benefits)
- **Warning Red**: #F44336 (Problems)
- **Info Blue**: #2196F3 (Information)

### Gradients:
- **Hero**: Navy (#1A1A2E) → Black (#000000)
- **Success**: Green (#4CAF50) → Teal (#009688)
- **CTA**: Orange (#FF9800) → Red (#F44336)

## 📐 Technical Specifications

### LinkedIn Image Sizes:
- **Single Image Post**: 1200x628px
- **Carousel Slides**: 1080x1080px
- **Story Format**: 1080x1920px
- **Profile Banner**: 1584x396px

### Typography:
- **Headlines**: Bold, Sans-serif (Montserrat/Arial Black)
- **Body Text**: Clean, Readable (Open Sans/Arial)
- **Code**: Monospace (Consolas/Monaco)

### Animation Effects:
- **Slide Transitions**: Smooth fade/slide
- **Icon Animations**: Gentle pulse/glow
- **Button States**: Scale on hover
- **Text Reveal**: Typewriter effect for key points
